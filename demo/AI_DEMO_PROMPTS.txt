RECOMMENDED PROMPT FOR DEMO (Best for showcasing AI capabilities)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

"Optimize 75 deliveries across Manchester with 8 vehicles, ensuring
each driver works no more than 8 hours. Balance the workload evenly
across all vehicles."

Why this prompt works well for demo:
- Shows natural language understanding
- Demonstrates constraint handling (8-hour limit)
- Triggers workload balancing recommendation
- Produces meaningful AI commentary on the results

WEATHER-AWARE ROUTING PROMPTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

These prompts demonstrate the weather integration feature:

1. BASIC WEATHER CHECK:
   "Plan 50 deliveries in London considering current weather conditions"

2. SAFETY FOCUSED:
   "Optimize routes for 100 stops in Manchester with weather safety scoring"

3. ADVERSE CONDITIONS:
   "Route 30 urgent deliveries in Scotland during rainy conditions with safety buffers"

4. EV NETWORK WEATHER:
   "Schedule visits to 25 Sky EV charging stations with weather-adjusted travel times"

5. REGIONAL WEATHER:
   "Plan 80 deliveries across Yorkshire checking for adverse weather warnings"

6. STORM RESPONSE:
   "Emergency routing for 40 service calls in North West England during storm conditions"

Expected AI Response includes:
- Weather Assessment: Clear/Minor/Moderate/Adverse/Severe
- Temperature range across route
- Wind speed (average and maximum)
- Travel time adjustment percentage
- Safety score (0-100)
- Specific recommendations for drivers


FOLLOW-UP PROMPTS (After initial optimization)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

After showing results, you can ask follow-up questions like:

"Can you reduce the number of vehicles used?"

"What if I add 20 more urgent deliveries?"

"Show me the longest route and suggest how to shorten it"


PROMPTS TO AVOID DURING DEMO
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Avoid these as they may produce less visual results:

- Very small numbers (under 20 stops) - results too simple
- Numbers over 1000 - may take longer than comfortable for video
- Vague requests without stop counts - AI may ask clarifying questions
- Complex multi-day scenarios - beyond current POC scope
